One of a kind is a children's clothing company started by a new mother who had a desire to create something "special" for her little girl. She has always had a love of creating one-of-a-kind pieces from custom pillows to party decorations. Her daughter has stirred that child like creativity in her and has been the inspiration behind all of her designs. Not long after, she decided to spread her colorful creations to fellow mothers so that they too could have something special for their little girl. Based out of her hometown of Houston, Texas, her desire is to provide quality girls clothing with a colorful flair that is also simple, pretty and modest.
