RETURNS/EXCHANGES
If the return is a result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item, etc.), we'll also pay the return shipping costs. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return your product within 15 days for a refund. Orders returned outside the 15-day return window without prior authorization will be donated to charity. Items marked FINAL SALE can only be exchanged and not returned. You should expect to receive your refund within 1-2 weeks of giving your package to the return shipper, however, in many cases you will receive a refund quicker. If you need to return or exchange an item, simply download the Returns Form below. Fill it out completely and send it in with the items you are returning. We'll notify you via e-mail of your refund once we've received and processed the returned item. For exchanges contact customerservice@cheekyplum.com to insure availability.

*If you do not fill out all of the information requested in the Returns Form we will not be able to process your return.


If you have any questions about returns or exchanges please email customerservice@cheekplum.com
